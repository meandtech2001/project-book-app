Ramdhari Singh Dinkar

Ramdhari Singh Dinkar was born on September 23, 1908, in Simaria village, Bengal Presidency, British India (now in Begusarai district in Bihar). He was educated at the University of Patna, where he studied history, philosophy, and political science. He also studied Sanskrit, Bengali, English, and Urdu.

Books Written by Ramdhari Singh Dinkar:

  1. Naye Subhaashit (1957)

  2. Rashmirathi (1952)
